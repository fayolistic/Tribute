In loving memory of late. chief Obafemi Jeremiah Awolowo. Born on march 6, 1909 and died on may 9, 1987.
Obafemi Awolowo was the first premier of the Western Region and later federal commissioner for finance, and vice chairman of the Federal Executive Council during the Nigerian Civil War.
Also, he was thrice a major contender for his country's highest office.He is a great Nigeria Nationalist and statesman.
